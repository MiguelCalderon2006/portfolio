Educational Opprotuniws

Dual Enrollment has been the greatest educational opportunity that I have had the privilege of taking advantage of. My school never offered any AP computer science classes so I decided to pursue the subject through dual enrollment. Taking advantage of this led to personal growth, unique opportunities, and allowed me to follow my passion.

Staying in dual enrollment was extremely challenging. I would get out of school at 4 then go to class at 6 for 4 hours. These days were really long and I couldn't get much free time at all. Through dual enrollment, I was able to take multiple core computer science courses. In one of the classes, I developed a C++ program with an interactive menu that gives the user the option to read a binary file containing periodic elements data, sorting them by atomic number, and then displaying them in an organized manner.

Dual enrollment allowed me to experience community college in person for the first time. This allowed me to network with college-level students, organized study sessions with them to keep my grades up. I sought out advice from my new friends from academics. I also got advice from my professor regarding school, computer science, and careers in general.
